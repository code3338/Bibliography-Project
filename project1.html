Biography:

Melissa Coppola

Hi, My name is Melissa Coppola. I was born in Columbia, MO. I have 5 brothers and two sisters. Of the 5 brothers, one of them is my twin brother. July 4th, 2015 I married a man that I am so blessed to have in my life.

I served in the United States Army Reserves for 9 years. While serving I completed my Associate of Arts degree in 2012, and then went on to complete my Bachelors of Health Science with a minor in Business from the University of Missouri - Columbia in 2016. After graduation, I worked as an Acute Case Management Consultant for Cerner Corporation where I traveled to client sites. While on site, I assisted clients in building and customizing Acute Case Management solutions geared to client preferences using Cerner's electronic build tool programs. Currently, I am expanding my career in web design and development, as I enjoy analyzing and solving problems,designing, and providing simple solutions to complex ideas and processes.

Regardless of my achievements, my greatest decision to date, is my decision to accept Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior of my life. I choose to serve Him faithfully. Though, I am far from perfect, I know my Heavenly Father,in the name of Jesus Christ, continues to help me grow, and to be better than I ever could be on my own.
